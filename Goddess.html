For My Goddess

Capricorn & Cancer

A Poem from Jacques

♋
Cancer
The Moon
❤️
♑
Capricorn
The Mountain
Our Digital Beginning
We met on social media 💻✨
this year that completely changed my heart ❤️.
Your photos… your deep gaze 🌊
felt like an invisible hand grabbing my soul 👐
a mysterious charm pulling me toward you 😍
before I even understood what was happening, Goddess 💖.
The more we wrote ✍️,
the more I fell for you 💘.
Every message brought me closer to your world 🌸
to your sweetness and hidden fire 🔥.
I desired you without even touching you 😏
as if your spirit was already caressing mine 💞.
The First Meeting
Then came that meeting… 💫
face to face.
The moment your body became real in front of mine 🤲
and everything changed ⚡.
Your energy, your presence, your smile 😍
hit me like a gentle shock 💓.
I was so happy to finally see you 😭
so excited too 😏,
because that day,
my body understood before my mind:
it didn't want to let go of yours 🤲💞.
It wanted to stay close,
stuck, drawn 😘
as if your skin was calling mine without a word 💕.
Magical Moments
We spent a magical moment ✨
a moment that vibrated in the air 🌬
a moment where my eyes slid over you
in silence 😍
as if I were discovering you
and devouring you at the same time 😏🔥.
When you went back home 🏠
you left a longing that burned softly 🔥
a longing that settled on my skin
like a vivid memory 💓.
The Realization
We met again once more ✨
and that day,
I realized it wasn't just a crush 💘
it was you I missed
physically, emotionally, instinctively 😭💞.
Since then, I haven't seen you…
and I feel your absence
like an empty space between my hands 🤲
like a space only your body can fill 🌙💖.
Your Jealousy, My Desire
You're jealous, yes 😅💗
a lot even.
But I love it 💘
because your jealousy
is your burning way of saying you want me 🔥
that you claim me
that you keep me for yourself 💕.
And me… Jacques 😏
I want to see you again 😍
hold you, feel your breath against mine 💞
hold you so tight that even time ⏳
wouldn't dare separate us 💓.
Since that month,
you marked me 🌹
and since you…
I want you, all of you 💖
and that moment when my body
didn't want to let go of yours 🤲🔥.
Cosmic Connection

🌙♑♋ Capricorn & Cancer 💌

And maybe it's no coincidence…
You, Goddess, are Cancer ♋ 🌊
a woman of the moon, of heart, of depth 💖
and me, Jacques, Capricorn ♑ 🏔
a man of earth, of strength, of steadiness 💪.
They say Cancer and Capricorn are opposites…
but opposites that attract 💘
that complement each other
that unite as if it was written ✨.
The moon lights up the mountain 🌙🏔
and the mountain protects the moon 🌄💖.
And since I met you,
I know that rare bond…
it's you, Goddess 💞🔥
I love you 😘❤️
- Jacques -
